Counseling

Psychosocial counseling is a calm, structured space to understand what is happening in your life and inside you. It can help you find steady next steps without having to “perform” or have everything figured out.

What we can work on

  • Life abroad and transitions
  • Stress and pressure
  • Relationships

How sessions work

  • Start: we clarify what feels most urgent right now
  • Understand: patterns, triggers, needs, and context
  • Tools: practical steps for everyday life (communication, boundaries, regulation)
  • Review: what is changing and what you want next

Practical details

  • Session length: 50 minutes
  • Pricing: € 90 per session
  • Cancellation: free up to 48 hours before*
  • Format: in-person and online

* In the event of a last-minute cancellation or failure to attend an appointment, the full amount will be charged.
